About Us

AIC Moisbridge is a Christ-centered church located in Moisbridge, Kenya. Our church has a rich history of community involvement, spiritual growth, and faithful service to God.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant community of believers living in obedience to God's Word and demonstrating His love in action.

Our Mission

To proclaim the Good News of Jesus Christ, disciple believers, and serve others through faith and compassion.
